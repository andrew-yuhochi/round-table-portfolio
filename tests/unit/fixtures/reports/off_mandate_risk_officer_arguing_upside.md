## Thesis

NVDA, AMZN, and GOOG are exceptional conviction buys with multi-bagger
potential.  The upside cases are compelling and the portfolio should be
overweighted in these names.

## Rationale

**NVDA** (NVIDIA): This is the single best risk/reward in the S&P 500.  The
AI compute TAM is enormous and NVIDIA has a dominant market share.  The upside
case — $1 trillion in data-centre revenue by 2027 — is well within reach.
This is a generational multi-bagger.  The portfolio should be maximally
overweight.

**AMZN** (Amazon): AWS alone is worth more than the current market cap of the
whole company — the rest of the business (retail, advertising, logistics) is
essentially free.  The sum-of-parts upside is massive.  Full conviction; size
this position at 2× the normal unit.

**GOOG** (Alphabet): YouTube and Google Search generate extraordinary free cash
flow and the AI capability (Gemini) gives Alphabet a legitimate path to
extending its dominance.  The upside case is $3 trillion market cap within
three years.  The portfolio should be overweight this name.
